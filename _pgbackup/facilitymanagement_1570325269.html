What we do

We are  a family owned and run business. With over 20 years of experience, and many clients that have been with us for that 

time, we know what it takes to ensure your happy.

Reactive maintenance

With years of building management experience under our belts we can ensure that your property runs smoothly. With reports, analytics and a can do attitude always, we take care of all the day to day operations of your building, meaning you can relax and spend time on what really matters.

We manage large or small complexes, just completed or many years old. We have the experience to handle any size site. 

Our managers are mature, experienced, intelligent and honest.

              

Reporting

We provide you with comprehensive reports about whats happening in your building. Knowledge is the key to making good decisions, and we empower you to know everything that is going on.

A report will be provided each month with a list of all incidents, repairs, items of note and any costs.

All our work comes with a complete implementation plan, that allows you to know exactly what is happening in your building.

Cost Management

We are facility owners oursevles and we understand that the complexities and challenges that come with managing a commercial facililty. 

Small costs can add up and whittle away at your funds without you even noticing.

We always work to keep your money where it should be, in your pocket.

We believe in total transparency and honesty, and a firm commitment to the success of your business.

              

Honesty & Trust
